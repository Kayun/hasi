Need to prepare for the HESI Specialty Exams?
Although most of our practice exams are geared towards the HESI Exit Exam, we do offer a limited amount of practice exams geared towards the HESI Specialty Exams.

- Fundamentals of Nursing.

- Pediatric Nursing (Nursing Care of Children).
- Mental Health.
- Community Health.
- Pharmacology.

- Nutrition.
- Nursing Leadership / Management.
- Pathophysiology.
- Gerontology.
- Health Assessment.
